ABOUT

Carlo Morelli is an actor based in Los Angeles. A graduate of NYU's Tisch School of the Arts with a BFA in Drama, he trained in the Meisner technique and has continued that study since returning to LA, most recently at the Baron Brown Studio. His film work spans lead and supporting roles across a run of independent features.

Off camera, Carlo splits his time between a seat at the American Cinematheque theaters and his own kitchen, where cooking is a close second craft. He's also a photographer, shooting both film and digital, and has cinematography and videography credits of his own.
